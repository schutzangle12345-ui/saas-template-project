The Problem We Solve

General-purpose AI tools often fall short in high-stakes technical environments. R&D managers face three critical roadblocks:

1. Technical Hallucinations: Standard LLMs misinterpret complex scientific data, leading to misguided experiments and wasted budgets.

2. Information Overload: Researchers spend endless nights skimming literature, which delays essential project milestones.

3. Fragmented Workflows: There is no unified workspace to connect literature summaries with active experiment plans, causing lost context.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between vast scientific knowledge and actionable experiment design, ensuring that innovation labs can move from hypothesis to discovery with 10x velocity and zero hallucinations.
