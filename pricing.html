Pricing FAQ

How do you handle Intellectual Property (IP)?

For Innovation Lab subscribers, we offer a Zero-Retention guarantee. Your queries, experimental data, and internal documentation are never used to train our models.

What happens after my 10 free queries?

The Explorer plan is designed for testing. Once you hit the limit, you can upgrade to the Innovation Lab plan for $200/month to continue your research without interruption.

Can we integrate with our internal lab systems?

Yes. Our Innovation Lab plan includes support for custom workflows and integrations with standard ELN (Electronic Lab Notebook) and LIMS tools via our robust API.
